# Output Template

Use this structure unless the user asks for a different format.

## Template

### 今日新闻池概览
- 用 1 到 3 段快速概括：今天的池子主要在发生什么，哪些领域最密集，哪些地方与用户画像高度匹配。

### 主线

For each mainline item:

- 报刊式标题
- 正文
- 事实锚点
- 选编说明

Voice:
- analytical
- causal
- concise

Title rule:
- the title should read like a publishable headline
- do not stuff the whole interpretation into the title
- let the body and selection note do the explaining

### 副刊 / 兴趣侧翼

For each supplement item:

- 报刊式标题
- 正文
- 事实锚点
- 为什么这条虽然不是最大的事件，但明显命中了用户的另一面

Voice:
- still publishable and edited
- but allow more domain-native tone than the mainline

### 兴趣雷达 / 未成线但值得看
Use this section for:

- hobby or fandom items that are important inside their own micro-domain
- travel / onsen / local culture items that are clearly on-profile but not large enough for a full supplement writeup
- small but high-signal interest hits that do not merge into a wider causal storyline

For each item:

- short headline
- one compact paragraph
- one sentence on why it matters inside that specific interest world

Voice:
- match the audience of that lane
- ACG can sound more scene-aware
- travel can sound more texture-rich
- photography can sound more enthusiast-facing

### 本轮新闻池覆盖不足
- 哪些兴趣方向本轮缺货
- 为什么判断是“池子没货”而不是“系统漏选”
- 用户未来可以考虑补什么类型的信息源

## Language rule

All sections should read as natural edited Chinese.

Avoid:
- translationese
- shorthand note fragments
- self-talk wording

Weak:
- `《序》CGI 制作秘话再被翻出来讲`

Better:
- `《序》的CG制作内幕又被周年节目重新带热`
- `《序》幕后制作往事再度成为周年讨论焦点`
