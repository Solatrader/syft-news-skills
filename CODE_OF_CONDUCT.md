# Code of Conduct

This repository aims to be practical, collaborative, and safe for contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and specific in feedback
- assume good intent while still being clear about problems
- focus criticism on ideas, artifacts, and workflows, not people
- share reproducible details when reporting issues
- avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

- abusive or hostile language
- harassment or discrimination
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- trolling, baiting, or repeated bad-faith escalation

## Maintainer Guidance

Maintainers may remove or edit content, close discussions, or block contributors whose behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

If you need to report a conduct issue, use the contact path in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) or the repository owner's preferred contact channel.
