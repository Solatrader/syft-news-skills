# What Is Syft AI

Syft AI is an AI-powered news product organized around followed topics, source-aware story pools, and a more personal reading surface than a generic one-size-fits-all feed.

![Syft AI app overview](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Solatrader/syft-cli-skills/main/assets/app.jpg)

## What Makes It Different

At a glance, the product view suggests a few important product ideas:

- the user follows topics, not just sources
- each topic can pull from multiple sources
- the feed is segmented by interest lane instead of flattened into one stream
- the system is designed to help users track both major events and niche personal interests

## Why That Matters

Most news workflows break down in one of two ways:

- they over-prioritize globally large stories and erase personal interests
- they over-personalize into isolated fragments and lose the structural big picture

Syft AI is interesting because it tries to preserve both:

- macro and structural developments
- identity-defining side interests

That balance is exactly why a skill layer is useful. Once the CLI exposes the topic graph and story pool, an agent can turn them into artifacts that are easier to read, share, and maintain.

## Where The Skills Fit

This repository does not replace Syft AI.
It builds on top of it.

The skills convert the Syft surfaces into:

- reusable profile files
- daily briefing artifacts
- trunk-branch storyline pages
- backfill memos and updated timelines
- persistent editorial guidance
